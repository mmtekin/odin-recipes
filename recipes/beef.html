Ground Beef

Ingredients

  • 1 lb 93 7 ground beef
  • Soy Sauce
  • Worchestershire Sauce
  • Sesame Oil
  • Ginger paste
  • Green Onion
  • Garlic

Steps

  1. Cook beef recipe on medium heat for total of 9 minutes.
  2. Heat the pan to medium heat.
  3. Cook beef for 7 minutes.
  4. After draining, add soy sauce, Worcestershire sauce, light sesame oil (a decent amount), and a good amount of ginger (3 pastes) to the pan while beef is still in the drainer
  5. Add cut-up garlic to the pan.
  6. Add beef back in and cook for longer till sauce fully reduces
  7. Plate it with rice
