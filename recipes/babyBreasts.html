Baby Chicken Breast

Ingredients

  • 1 lb Baby Chicken Breast
  • Garlic Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Black Pepper
  • Smoked Chipotle
  • Avocado oil

Steps

Total cook time is 13 minutes or internal temp = 165 Â°F

  1. Heat pan for one below max for 3 minutes
  2. Season chicken as follows
  3. Salt
  4. Garlic Powder (light)
  5. Onion Powder (light)
  6. Black Pepper (Heavy)
  7. Smoked Chipotle (light)
  8. Toss and mix until chicken fully covered
  9. Cook on heat level 6 until 6 minutes and then turn it down to 4 or 5
  10. Flip every 3 minutes
