French Toast

Ingredients

  • 1 egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup milk
  • 4 slices bread

Steps

  1. Beat egg, vanilla, and cinnamon in dish then stir in milk.
  2. Get your pan to medium heat with some butter in there to keep it from sticking.
  3. Dip bread, turning to coat both sides.
  4. Toss in hot pan and cook for about 3-4 minutes per side.
  5. Serve and enjoy! I eat mine with a liberal coating of cinnamon sugar.
